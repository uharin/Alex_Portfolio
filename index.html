WURLD

WURLD is a proof of concept for an environmental impact app that provides users with a way to track their positive contributions to the environment and to engage in friendly competition with others in their area. Through gamification, WURLD seeks to create a community where like-minded individuals can help and inspire each other to make environmentally-conscious decisions. This app was built from the ground up utilizing a VueJS frontend, a Rails backend, and a PostGresSQL database.
